Jaisalmer Pack

Overview

A place where the landscape turns barren and drier, vegetation thins, and rippled dunes appear in all their sandy glory, Rajasthan’s golden city Jaisalmer is home to India’s only living fort. Adorned with larger-than-life havelis and carved temples, Jaisalmer is an amalgam of exotic Indian desert culture, heritage, and adventure. Named after Maharaja Jaisal Singh, Jaisalmer is one of the most popular historical places not only in Rajasthan but in the entire country. For the traveler in you keen on visiting a location with a rich heritage, religious significance, and of course delicious Rajasthani food, you must plan a trip to Jaisalmer.

  • 04 Days / 03 Nights
  • Age 12+
  • Jaiselmer

Itinerary

Day 1 - Journey Begins

Assemble at the boarding point in Gurgaon - IFFCO Chowk.

Start an overnight journey to Jaisalmer and socialize with your co-travellers.

Day 2 - Thar Desert Diaries

Enroute Jaisalmer we will take a pit stop for a quick refill.
After arriving in Thar Desert, check-in to our campsite located in Sam Sand Dunes, freshen-up and have some leisure.

Post refreshment, we will head out for Camel Safari in Thar Desert. Witness the beautiful sunset while enjoying your camel ride. Experience the thrill in Jeep Dune Bashing.
In the evening, enjoy a warm bonfire with Rajasthani folk music, Kalbelia dance and mouth-watering snacks.
Savour home-style cooked Rajasthani dinner and overnight stay in camps.

Day 3 - The Longewala Battlefield

Wake up early in the morning and post breakfast check-out from our campsite.
Visit the abandoned Kuldhara village. Travel to Longewala and visit the war memorial stationed near the India-Pakistan border; gather for an #InstaPerfect group picture. Enroute Longewala Post visit the famous Tanot Mata Temple (if time permits).

After arriving in Jaisalmer city, check-in to our hotel, freshen-up and have some leisure.
Savour home-style cooked dinner and overnight stay in a hotel in Jaisalmer.

Day 4 - Rajasthan’s Golden City

Wake up early in the morning and post breakfast check-out from our hotel.
Head out to explore India’s only living fort - Jaisalmer Fort. Take a walk in this magnificent historic fort and learn the legends associated with it.
Visit other major attractions like Gadsisar Lake, Patwon Ki Haveli, etc.

Feel free to explore the local market and taste different cuisines at recommended themed cafes/restaurants.
In the evening, start-off your return journey to Gurgaon.
