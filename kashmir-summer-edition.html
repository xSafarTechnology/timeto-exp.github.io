Kashmir Summer Edition

Overview

Kashmir is a valley rich in culture and natural wonders as well as warm hospitality. Kashmir offers unique experiences for tourists in the form of spicy indigenous dishes accompanied by a variety of teas, hiking or trekking experiences in the many mountains of the region, admiring the houseboats Relax on Lake Srinagar and visiting the many temples, and shrines, and religious and spiritual centers found throughout the state. If you enjoy collecting memorabilia from the places you visit, you can own beautiful Pashmina silks, carpets, and other handmade textiles made by indigenous Kashmiri artisans..

  • 06 Days / 05 Nights
  • Age 12+
  • Kashmir

Itinerary

Day 1 - Srinagar Arrival | The Dal Lake Diaries

  • After arriving in Srinagar, pick-up from the airport on a sharing basis (before 12pm only).
  • Drive towards Dal Lake, check-in to your cozy houseboat and freshen-up.
  • Quickly head out for local exploration in Srinagar - the famous Mughal Gardens including the Nishat Bagh (the garden of pleasure) and the Shalimar Bagh (abode of love), situated on the banks of serene Dal Lake.
  • In the evening, treat yourself with jaw dropping sunset scenes over the Dal Lake and feel the Kashmiriyat during the beautiful Shikara ride in Dal.
  • In the evening, return to your houseboat in Dal Lake. Savour home-style cooked dinner and overnight stay in a houseboat.

Day 2 - Sonamarg Exploration | Srinagar Stay

  • Wake up to a peaceful mountain morning and post a delightful breakfast, begin your drive from Srinagar to full day excursion of Sonamarg.
  • Sonamarg is a destination with full of mesmerising views, located at the height of 8960 Ft.
  • You may take a pony ride (at your own cost) to Thajiwas Glacier where snow
  • remains round the year.
  • In the evening, return to your hotel in Srinagar.
  • Savour home-style cooked dinner and overnight stay in a hotel.

Day 3 - Srinagar To Gulmarg | Gondola Ride

  • Wake up early and post breakfast, begin your drive from Srinagar towards Gulmarg (World’s highest green golf course).
  • After arriving in Gulmarg, experience Asia’s highest Gondola ride in Gulmarg (on your own) and get mesmerized by the stunning landscapes from Gulmarg to Kongdoori and Marry Shoulder.
  • In the evening, return to your hotel in Tangmarg/Srinagar.
  • Savour home-style cooked dinner and overnight stay in a hotel.

Day 4 - Drive To Pahalgam | Exploration

  • Wake up to a peaceful mountain morning and post a delightful breakfast, begin your drive from Tangmarg towards Pahalgam via Drung waterfall.
  • Take a halt at the majestic Drung Waterfall that remains frozen during winters and thus makes an interesting spot while traveling to Pahalgam.
  • After arriving in Pahalgam, check-in to our comfy hotel and have some leisure.
  • Explore the gorgeous Betab Valley, Chandanwari and Aru Valley nestled in Pahalgam. Get awestruck by the beautiful views of mighty snow-clad mountain peaks and indulge in various adventure activities (on your own).
  • Savour home-style cooked dinner and overnight stay in a hotel.

Day 5 - Explore Srinagar | Temple, Markets & More

  • Wake up to a peaceful mountain morning and post a delightful breakfast, drive back to Srinagar and check-in to our comfy hotel.
  • Visit the popular Shankaracharya Temple and witness the beautiful view of Kashmir Valley as seen from Takht-e-Suleiman hill.
  • Visit other major attractions in Srinagar such as Pari Mahal, the holy Hazaratbal Shrine and Jama Masjid to name a few.
  • In the evening, spend some time near Dal Lake and try out the authentic flavours of Kashmiri Wazwan in the local market or simply go for cafe hopping (on your own).
  • In the evening, return to your hotel in Srinagar. Savour home-style cooked dinner and overnight stay in a hotel.

Day 6 - Goodbye Kashmir | Trip Ends

  • In the morning, say goodbye to your hosts in Srinagar and leave for the airport. You’ll be dropped at Srinagar airport.
  • Reach home with lots of amazing memories and unforgettable experiences.
